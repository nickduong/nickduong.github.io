The Wonders of Ocean Life (Climate Change)

Life in the Arctic and Southern Oceans thrives despite extreme conditions, but climate change is disrupting the ecosystems. The loss of sea ice harms species like polar bears, walruses, and pteropods. Communities that are near the poles face coastal erosion, permafrost, and forced relocations due to the changes in the environment. If you're interested in learning more, visit Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History.
